Deli Snack Box

Description

Prep for the week ahead with these healthy, budget-friendly snack boxes! High protein, high fiber and so nutritious!

I love taking these snack boxes out and about with me for the day.

They provide a high protein, high fiber and nutritious snack anytime throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces thinly sliced turkey breast
  • 1 large egg, hard boiled
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 ounce sharp cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 4 pita bites crackers
  • 2 tablespoons raw almonds

Steps

  1. Place turkey, egg, tomatoes, cheese, crackers and almonds into meal prep containers.
